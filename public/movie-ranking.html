The best movies of all time according to me

My current favorites

Scream

This movie series is so good! Classic dumb blonde horror movie. The first movie follows high schooler Sidney Prescott and her friends as their small town is terrorized by a masked killer, with everyone a suspect. It's widely regarded as a modern horror classic worth watching for fans of suspense and dark comedy.

The live action Cinderella

The movie follows Ella, a kind young women whose life changes after meeting a mysterious stranger.

The Pink Panther

The Pink Panther is a stylish comedy caper about the bumbling Inspector Jacques Clousea's pursuit of a suave jewel theif bent of stealing the priceless "Pink Panther" diamond.
